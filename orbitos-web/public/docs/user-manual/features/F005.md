# Business Canvas

Design and iterate on your business model using canvas templates.

## Overview

The Canvas feature provides:
- **Business Model Canvas** - The classic 9-block template
- **Lean Canvas** - For startups and new ventures
- **Value Proposition Canvas** - Focus on customer value
- **Custom templates** - Create your own

## Creating a Canvas

1. Navigate to **Canvases** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Canvas**
3. Choose a template type
4. Enter a name and scope
5. Start filling in blocks

## Business Model Canvas Blocks

### Customer Segments
Who are your most important customers?
- Mass market, niche, segmented, diversified

### Value Propositions
What value do you deliver?
- Newness, performance, customization, design

### Channels
How do you reach customers?
- Direct, indirect, owned, partner

### Customer Relationships
What type of relationship?
- Personal assistance, self-service, automated

### Revenue Streams
How do you earn money?
- Asset sale, subscription, licensing, advertising

### Key Resources
What do you need?
- Physical, intellectual, human, financial

### Key Activities
What must you do?
- Production, problem solving, platform/network

### Key Partners
Who helps you?
- Strategic alliances, suppliers, joint ventures

### Cost Structure
What are your costs?
- Fixed, variable, economies of scale/scope

## Tips

- Start with customer segments and value propositions
- Use sticky notes for brainstorming
- Review and update regularly
- Share with stakeholders for feedback

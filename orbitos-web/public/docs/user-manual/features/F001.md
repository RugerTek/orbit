# Getting Started with OrbitOS

Welcome to OrbitOS, your AI-native operating system for business operations.

## Quick Overview

OrbitOS helps you manage your organization's:
- **People & Structure** - Team members, roles, and org charts
- **Processes** - Visual workflow design and management
- **Goals** - OKRs and objective tracking
- **Resources** - Organizational assets and capabilities

## Navigation

Use the sidebar on the left to navigate between sections. The main areas include:

- **Overview** - Dashboard with key metrics
- **Canvases** - Business model and strategic planning
- **Goals** - Objectives and key results
- **People** - Team member management
- **Org Chart** - Visual organization hierarchy
- **Roles** - Define responsibilities
- **Functions** - Atomic work units
- **Processes** - Workflow design
- **Resources** - Asset management
- **AI Agents** - Configure AI assistants

## Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Cmd+K` / `Ctrl+K` - Open help search
- `Escape` - Close dialogs and panels
- `?` - Show keyboard shortcuts

## Getting Help

Press `Cmd+K` anytime to search for help on any topic. You can also click the help button in the bottom-right corner.

# Goals & OKRs

Track objectives and key results to align your organization.

## Overview

The Goals feature helps you:
- **Set objectives** for teams and individuals
- **Define key results** to measure progress
- **Track completion** over time
- **Cascade goals** through the organization

## Creating a Goal

1. Navigate to **Goals** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Goal**
3. Enter the objective title
4. Add measurable key results
5. Set a target date

## Objectives

A good objective should be:
- **Inspiring** - Motivates the team
- **Qualitative** - Describes what you want to achieve
- **Time-bound** - Has a clear deadline
- **Actionable** - Team can influence the outcome

### Example Objectives
- "Become the leading provider in our market"
- "Delight our customers with exceptional service"
- "Build a world-class engineering team"

## Key Results

Key results measure progress toward objectives:
- **Quantitative** - Include numbers
- **Measurable** - Can be tracked objectively
- **Achievable** - Challenging but realistic
- **Time-bound** - Match the objective timeline

### Example Key Results
- "Increase customer satisfaction score from 7.5 to 9.0"
- "Reduce churn rate from 5% to 2%"
- "Ship 3 major features per quarter"

## Tracking Progress

- Update key results regularly
- Review in team meetings
- Adjust targets if needed
- Celebrate achievements
